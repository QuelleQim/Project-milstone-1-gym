Find us


We are located in newly built premises at Storseglet 23. You can easily find us right by the train station, Vega station.

Find us by commuter train

Either take the train from T-Centralen towards Nynäshamn or Västerhaninge and jump off at Vega station after approximately 24 minutes.

Find us by car

Or take the car from the city central and travel by route 73 towards Nynäshamn and take the "trafikplats Vega" exit and take a right in the first and second roundabout. And then after a one minute drive the box will be on your right! Parking is accesible on the left hand side by the train tracks.
