About us

Here at Stux Crossbox we strive for quality, equality and passion. We love what we do and hope to inspire more people to live a healthier lifestyle. The most important assets we have are our members, which is why we reguarly evaluate, analyse and improve our box based on customer reviews. For example, our members expressed a need to bring along their dogs to the box. So we hired a walker so that our members can work out without worrying about their precious pups!

Why crossfit?

Crossfit is a fitness regime with a main focus on strength and conditioning mixed together with diffrent sports like olympic lifting, aerobics and calisthenics. By combining diffrent focal points as strength, conditioning but also flexibility, stamina, power, speed, balance, accuracy, agility and coordination we get a varied, fun and challenging exercise i which we apply our whole body to become the best version of ourselfes.
