Trainers
Honorine

Head Coach
Founder
Level 2 trainer

Honorine is the founder of Stux Crossbox. They started training as a young child at the age of five doing different sports, but eventually at the age of seven they devoted all of their time to gymnastics and ended up competing for many years. At the age of 21 they went to a crossfit box for a free trial and has never stopped since. Now they hold a level 2 certification. Honorine is passionate about training crossfit and wants to spread the message about it so that more people can enjoy a great workout and live a healthier lifestyle. To do this they often host and partake in seminars and lectures about training and psysical health. They are also considered jack of trades, master of none due to their expansive education experience. While they are a certified level 2 crossfit trainer, Honorine is also a certified nutritionist, gardener and car mechanic.

Martin

Coach
Co-Founder, personal trainer & yoga instructor
Level 1 trainer

Martin is the co-founder and childhood friend of Honorine. Martin holds a level 1 certification, is an educated personal trainer and certified yoga instructor. He has been doing crossfit for over ten years and before that he competed in olympic weightlifting. Martins favorite interests are crossfit, baking and dogs. He owns three dogs: Blossom, Bubbles, and Buttercup. They are kind of the unofficial mascots of Stux Crossbox and likes to greet everyone at the entrance with a smile.

Sara

Assisting coach
Naprapath & aspiring personal trainer

Sara is Stux Crossbox's naprapath and currently studying to become a personal trainer. Sara has a long record of various training and has lived in Canada playing professional hockey. She is a master of treating and rehabiliting athletical injuries and is available at the box for rehab bookings and athletical massages.
